# Vision

PrepOS is an AI-powered Study Operating System designed to help students prepare for competitive examinations through intelligent planning, structured learning, performance analysis, and personalized guidance.

Unlike traditional study planners or note-taking applications, PrepOS combines planning, learning, revision, analytics, mock analysis, and AI into one unified platform.

The long-term goal is to become the most comprehensive study platform for students worldwide.

---

# Mission

Help every student stay consistent throughout their preparation journey by providing a single platform where they can plan, study, revise, analyze, and improve.

PrepOS should remove the need to switch between multiple applications like Google Calendar, Notion, Excel, Anki, Todoist, and separate note-taking tools.

Everything should exist inside one ecosystem.

---

# Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive exams often struggle because their preparation is fragmented across multiple applications.

Common problems include:

* No centralized study planner.
* Notes scattered across different platforms.
* Formula sheets difficult to revise.
* Mock analysis performed manually.
* No structured error tracking.
* Weak revision planning.
* No adaptive study schedule.
* Poor progress visualization.
* Lack of long-term consistency.

These issues reduce productivity and increase mental overhead.

PrepOS solves these problems by integrating all study-related workflows into a single intelligent application.

# Core Principles

PrepOS follows six core principles.

## 1. Simplicity

The application should remain easy to use despite offering advanced features.

Every important action should require minimal clicks.

---

## 2. Intelligence

The application should understand the student's preparation.

It should recommend what to study instead of simply storing information.

---

## 3. Automation

Preparation should be automatically organized.

Roadmaps, reminders, revision schedules, and analytics should require minimal manual effort.

---

## 4. Consistency

The system should encourage regular study through streaks, reminders, progress tracking, and adaptive planning.

---

## 5. Personalization

Every student's study plan should be unique.

Schedules should adapt according to:

* Available study hours
* College schedule
* Gym schedule
* Mock performance
* Weak topics
* Target exam date

---

## 6. Scalability

PrepOS should support additional examinations without major architectural changes.

Adding a new exam should require only syllabus and roadmap configuration.

# Product Philosophy

PrepOS is not merely a study planner.

It is an intelligent operating system that helps students make better decisions throughout their preparation journey.

Every feature should answer one question:

**"Does this help the student prepare more effectively?"**

If the answer is no, the feature should not be included.
